VEHICLE CRIME IN LEICESTER

Most vehicle crime is preventable. It can take as little as 10 seconds for a thief to steal something from your car. The best way to protect your belongings is to lock your car whenever you leave it.

Where you park can make a big difference to the safety of your car and your belongings. Look out for car parks approved by the police Safer Parking (opens in a new window) scheme. You can find them by looking for their distinctive 'Park Mark' signs.

Prevention Tips

  • Remove valuables from your vehicle – leave it on show expect it to go!
  • Remove Sat Navs, including support cables.
  • Ensure doors are locked, windows are closed, and keys are removed when leaving the car, even if it’s only for a moment!
  • Park your vehicle in a well-lit spot visible to others or covered by CCTV.
  • Register any vehicle equipment such as CD players, in-car DVD player and Sat Navs for free at immobilise.com.
